About Conference

This will be a three-day Conference on Computational Science and Mathematical Modelling organized by Department of Mathematics, School of Advanced Sciences (SAS), VIT-AP University in collaboration with University of KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. This conference is intended for the participants who aspire to excel in aforementioned fields. The goal of this conference is to bring top researchers, industry personnel, and faculty members in this area to VIT-AP University for fostering collaboration and to manifest scholars for important problems in the growing field. The meeting will stimulate joint work among researchers both in India and abroad. It provides an opportunity to explore the recent challenges and developments in the area of Mathematical Modelling, Computational Science, Engineering, Computational Biology, and Mathematical Chemistry.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions on a wide range of research topics, spanning both theoretical and computational area. The topics of interest include, but are not limited to:
Computational Science and Mathematical Modelling

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Wave Mechanics
  • Nonlinear Dynamic Problems
  • Differential Equations
  • Difference Equations
  • Integral Equations
  • Fractional Differential Equation
  • Computational Uncertainties
  • Numerical Optimization
  • Numerical Analysis
  • Perturbation Techniques
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Computational Biology
  • Mathematical Physics
  • Mathematical Chemistry

Data Science

  • Data Mining
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Data Engineering and Pipeline Design
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Deep Learning
  • Supervised and Unsupervised Learning
  • Quantum ML
  • Generative Models
